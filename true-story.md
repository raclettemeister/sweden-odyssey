# The Sweden Odyssey - True Story

## The Group

- **Scout Unit**: La 90ème (La Nonantième), Compagnie Scout Notre-Dame de Stockel
- **Members**: 20 boy scouts, aged 16 to 18
- **Staff**: 3 adults

## The Departure

The journey began in **Wezembeek-Oppem**, Belgium, where the group assembled and prepared their huge backpacks for the trip ahead.
